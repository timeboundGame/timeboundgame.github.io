🕒 Timebound – Game Info

🔥 How the Game Works

In Timebound, your goal is to keep your remaining time from running out. You can gain time by winning mini-games and lose time if you fail. Every second counts!

✅ If you win 🏆 → Your time increases.

❌ If you lose → Your time decreases.

You must survive by playing mini-games and keeping your timer above 0!

🎮 Game Categories

♠️ Speed & Reaction - Test your reflexes and reaction speed!
♦️ Trick & Deception - Beware of hidden rules and mind games!
♣️ Logic & Puzzle - Use your brain to solve tricky challenges!
♥️ Coming Soon! - A new challenge is on its way...

⚠️ Remember: If your timer reaches 0, you lose the game. Can you stay in the game and beat the challenges?
